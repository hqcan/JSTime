Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2025
Privacy Commitment: JSTime is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Event Data: Countdown events, reminders and related content you create
  • Settings Preferences: App settings, theme choices, notification preferences, etc.
  • Feedback Information: Opinions and suggestions you provide through in-app feedback features

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Statistics: App usage frequency, feature usage (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: App crash logs (without personal data)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Run core app functions, sync your data
  • Improve Experience: Optimize app performance, fix issues, develop new features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests
  • Security Protection: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

Your event data is primarily stored on your device, and we cannot directly access this information.

3.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

  • If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your event data will be synced through Apple's iCloud service
  • iCloud data is protected by Apple's privacy policy, and we cannot access your iCloud data
  • You can turn off iCloud sync at any time in settings

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to third parties, except when:

  • With your explicit consent
  • Legal Requirements: Complying with laws, court orders or government requirements
  • Security Needs: Protecting our rights, property or safety, and the safety of other users

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple Services

  • App Store: App downloads and subscription management
  • iCloud: Optional data sync service
  • Push Notifications: Reminder and notification services

5.2 Analytics Services

We may use anonymized analytics services to understand app usage and help improve the product. These services do not collect information that can identify you.

6. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Local Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted and stored on your device
  • Transmission Security: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Control: Strictly limit access to user data
  • Regular Review: Regularly review and update security measures

7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access Right: Know what information we have collected about you
  • Correction Right: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion Right: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability Right: Obtain your data in a commonly used format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will immediately delete the relevant information.

9. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while you use the app
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data retained for up to 24 months
  • Crash Reports: Deleted immediately after fixing issues
  • Deletion Requests: Processed within 30 days of receiving the request

10. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in locations outside your country/region. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and take appropriate safeguards.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • App Store description updates

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

Feedback Method: Please search for "JSTime" in the App Store and leave a comment on the app page. We will respond promptly to address your concerns

App Name: JSTime

Response Time: We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days

13. Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of [Your Country/Region]. If you reside in the EU, you are also protected by GDPR.
