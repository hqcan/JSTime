Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025
Important Notice: This privacy policy is specifically designed to comply with Apple App Store subscription app requirements, ensuring transparency and user rights protection.

1. Overview

Welcome to JSTime. We understand the importance of user privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Event Data: Countdown events, reminders and related content you create
  • Settings Preferences: App settings, theme choices, notification preferences, etc.
  • Feedback Information: Opinions and suggestions you provide through in-app feedback features

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Statistics: App usage frequency, feature usage (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: App crash logs (without personal data)

2.3 Subscription Information

  • Purchase Data: Subscription status, purchase receipts (processed through Apple)
  • Billing Information: Subscription start/end dates, renewal status
  • Apple ID Integration: Subscription management through Apple's systems

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • User-created event content and data
  • Location information
  • Contact information

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected limited information only for:

  • Provide Services: Run core app functions, sync your data
  • Improve Experience: Optimize app performance, fix issues, develop new features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests
  • Security Protection: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse
  • Subscription Management: Verify subscription status, enable premium features, process renewals
  • Payment Processing: Handle subscription billing through Apple's secure payment system
  • App Analytics: Analyze app usage trends
  • Ensure Security: Maintain application security

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your event data is securely stored locally on your device, and we cannot access this data.

4.2 iCloud Sync (Premium Feature)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync functionality, your data will be synchronized through Apple's iCloud service. We cannot access any data stored in iCloud, and all data transfers are handled securely by Apple.

  • Data synced through Apple's secure iCloud service
  • We have no access to your iCloud-stored data
  • Can be disabled anytime in app settings
  • Protected by Apple's privacy policy

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to third parties, except when:

  • With your explicit consent
  • Legal Requirements: Complying with laws, court orders or government requirements
  • Security Needs: Protecting our rights, property or safety, and the safety of other users

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may include the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases
  • Apple CloudKit: For iCloud data synchronization

These services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Subscription and Payment

7.1 App Store Subscription Terms

  • Subscription Price: Annual subscription fee is $19.99 USD
  • Auto-Renewal: Subscription will automatically renew 24 hours before expiration unless you cancel beforehand
  • Manage Subscription: You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your device settings
  • Refund Policy: Refunds are managed by Apple App Store policy
  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed securely through Apple's payment system
  • Billing Information: We do not store your credit card or payment information

7.2 How to Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

  1. Open "Settings" on your iOS device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "JSTime" and tap on it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Important: You must cancel at least 24 hours before your subscription renews to avoid the next billing cycle.

7.3 Subscription Data Privacy

  • Purchase Receipts: We only process necessary receipt data for subscription verification
  • Billing History: Your complete billing history is available through your Apple ID account
  • No Financial Data: We never have access to your credit card or banking information
  • Subscription Status: We only track whether your subscription is active or inactive

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Apple Services

  • App Store: App downloads and subscription management
  • StoreKit: In-app purchase and subscription processing
  • iTunes Account: Payment processing and subscription billing
  • iCloud: Optional data sync service
  • Push Notifications: Reminder and notification services

8.2 Analytics Services

We may use anonymized analytics services to understand app usage and help improve the product. These services do not collect information that can identify you.

9. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Local Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted and stored on your device
  • Transmission Security: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Control: Strictly limit access to user data
  • Regular Review: Regularly review and update security measures

10. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access Right: Know what information we have collected about you
  • Correction Right: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion Right: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability Right: Obtain your data in a commonly used format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Subscription Rights: View, modify, or cancel your subscription at any time through Apple ID settings
  • Payment Transparency: Access complete records of subscription charges and billing history

11. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

12. Data Retention

We only retain collected information for the time necessary for the purposes specified in this policy. When information is no longer needed, we securely delete it.

  • Account Data: Retained while you use the app
  • Subscription Data: Retained for the duration of subscription plus 1 year for billing and support purposes
  • Purchase Records: Maintained according to Apple's receipt validation requirements
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data retained for up to 24 months
  • Crash Reports: Deleted immediately after fixing issues
  • Deletion Requests: Processed within 30 days of receiving the request

13. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in locations outside your country/region. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and take appropriate safeguards.

14. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • App Store description updates
  • Subscription renewal notifications (for subscription-related changes)
  • Updated policy pages

Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

App Support: https://hqcan.github.io/JSTime/support.html

Response Time: We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days

Subscription Support: For subscription-related inquiries, include your Apple ID and purchase date
