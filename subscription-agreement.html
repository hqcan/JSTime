Premium Annual Subscription Agreement

Last Updated: June 1, 2025
Auto-Renewal Subscription Service Description
This agreement governs your use of JSTime Premium annual subscription service.

1. Subscription Service

Service Name: JSTime Premium Annual Subscription (Auto-Renewable)

Subscription Period: 1 Year (12 months)

Service Type: Continuous annual subscription

2. Subscription Pricing

Price: $19.99 USD per year

Equivalent: Approximately $1.67 USD per month

Auto-Renewal: $19.99 USD annually, can be canceled anytime

Effective Date: Starts from purchase confirmation date

3. Payment Confirmation

  • Subscription is managed by the user
  • Payment is charged to your iTunes Account upon purchase confirmation
  • All transactions are processed through Apple's secure payment system
  • Payment is processed using your iTunes Account credentials

4. Auto-Renewal Process

  • Your Apple iTunes Account will be charged within 24 hours before expiration
  • Upon successful payment, the subscription period extends by one subscription cycle
  • Renewal occurs automatically unless canceled
  • You will receive notification before renewal

5. Cancellation Instructions

How to Cancel Your Subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap on your name at the top
  3. Select "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "JSTime Premium" and tap on it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Important: If you don't cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the subscription period, it will automatically renew.

6. Premium Features Included

  • Unlimited event creation
  • Advanced reminder options
  • Multiple widget styles
  • Privacy protection with Face ID/Touch ID
  • Custom themes and app icons
  • iCloud sync across devices
  • Data export functionality
  • Priority customer support

7. Free Trial Terms

  • Trial Display: Free trial periods (if offered) will be clearly displayed before purchase
  • Auto-Conversion: Free trial automatically converts to paid subscription unless canceled
  • Cancellation: Cancel anytime during free trial to avoid charges
  • Full Access: Premium features are available during free trial period
  • No Trial Notice: Current subscription does not include a free trial period

8. Related Agreements

By subscribing, you also agree to:

  • User Agreement: Terms of Service

9. Subscription Management

  • View and manage subscriptions in iOS Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions
  • Change or cancel subscriptions anytime
  • Refunds handled according to Apple's refund policy
  • Contact us for subscription support
